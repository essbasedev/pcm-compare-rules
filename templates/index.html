PCM Tools

This page provides the tools (as of now there's only one) that are useful for essbase developers, especially for the PCM (HPCM Management Ledger or PCMCS in Oracle EPM Cloud) developers.

Compare Rules

You might be working on a PCM project where the rules are being changed frequently. But, there's no option to track what is being changed. You may find this tool useful, if you are in the state. You can compare two POVs and get the output by ruleset/rule.
